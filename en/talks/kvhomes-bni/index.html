The project's philosophy

Why we're doing it —
and how we put it into the city.

01 · Why we're doing it
An industrial city needs a livable inner core.
  • Kazincbarcika's 50+-year-old panel housing stock isn't the thing to reform — alongside it, we need to offer new-generation housing.
  • Young people and families working in the city have nowhere to move into — this is what we're fixing.
  • The KV Homes system delivers: 3–6 month lead time, A+++ energy, predictable price — exactly what Hungarian mid-sized cities lack.
02 · How we put it into the city
Kolor isn't decoration.
Character.
  • KolorCity DNA: colors, painted firewalls, motivational sculptures — not stuck on, but built into the buildings.
  • With local artists: the firewalls and park sculptures are by Kazincbarcika-based artists — the city speaks in its own voice.
  • Shared park, common space: ~15,500 m² of green between the houses, with bike storage and shared amenities — not just a place to live, a place to live in.
