Bitcoin education

Talks from the Bitcoin Miskolc 2026 conference (April 17–19, 2026, Hajdúszoboszló, Hungary). The goal is to convey how Bitcoin actually works — no hype, no technical jargon. Four talks at varying depth.

What is Bitcoin?

From the evolution of money to the White Paper. Genesis Block, the three eras of accounting, digital property (Kindle-Orwell, Cyprus), the three pillars, consensus ≠ democracy. The gateway deck — recommended entry point.
